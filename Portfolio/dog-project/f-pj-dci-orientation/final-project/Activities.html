Hours and days of our activities

Group puppy classes are possible on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 18:00 to 19:00 and every Saturday from 10:00 to 11:00 For private classes, the time and day must be agreed with us by phone call or email. In winter all lessons will take place indoors.

The training courses are available from Monday to Friday from 9.00 to 12.00 and from 14.00 to 16.00 by appointment and on Saturdays from 13.00 to 18.00 All courses are usually held in our camp training, in case of bad weather they will take place indoors.

The walks are usually organized on the second and fourth Sunday of the month. The departure times are different depending on the place where the walk will take place. If it will be necessary to travel by car, the various groups will be organized in advance.
